Meet the Doctors

Our experienced team is dedicated to helping you achieve optimal health through personalized chiropractic care.

Dr. Kevin Haselhorst, D.C.

Co-owner & Chiropractor

Born and raised in Puerto Rico, Dr. Kevin earned his Biology degree (with honors) from the University of Puerto Rico and his Doctor of Chiropractic from Life University in 2005. A former NCAA Division II collegiate basketball player who also played professionally in Puerto Rico, he specializes in structural alignment, spinal curve correction, exercise rehabilitation, and injury prevention.

Dr. Meggen Haselhorst, D.C.

Co-owner & Chiropractor

Originally from Delaware, Dr. Meggen holds a BS in Exercise Science from the University of Delaware and earned her Doctor of Chiropractic (with honors) from Life University in 2005. She holds a 14-module certification from the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association and specializes in care for pregnant women, infants, children, and families using the Webster Technique.
