What to Expect at Your First Visit

Your first appointment takes about 45 to 60 minutes. Here is what happens step by step.

1

Welcome & Paperwork

When you arrive, our friendly front desk team will greet you and help you complete a brief health history form. If you prefer, you can download and fill it out at home beforehand. Plan to arrive about 10 minutes early.

2

One-on-One Consultation

You will sit down with your doctor for an unhurried conversation about your health history, your current symptoms, your daily activities, and your goals. We listen first — this is your time to tell us what is going on.

3

Comprehensive Examination

Your doctor will perform a thorough physical exam including postural analysis, range of motion testing, orthopedic and neurological tests, and spinal palpation. We check everything so nothing gets missed.

4

Findings & Treatment Plan

Your doctor will explain what they found in plain language — no confusing jargon. You will understand your diagnosis and receive a personalized treatment plan with clear expectations for how long recovery should take and what it will cost.

5

Your First Adjustment

In most cases, you will receive your first chiropractic adjustment on the same day. Many patients feel improvement immediately. We will also send you home with any stretches or exercises to support your recovery between visits.

How to Prepare for Your Visit

  • Bring a photo ID and your insurance card (if using insurance)
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Arrive 10 minutes early to complete paperwork
  • Write down any questions you want to ask the doctor
  • Note your current medications and any supplements you take
  • Bring any relevant medical records or imaging from previous providers
  • Eat a light meal beforehand — do not come on an empty stomach
