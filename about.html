About Milestone Chiropractic Centre

Serving the Fredericksburg community with compassionate, hands-on chiropractic care since 2009.

Our Story

Milestone Chiropractic Centre was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: to provide Fredericksburg-area families with accessible, high-quality chiropractic care in a warm, welcoming environment. What started as a single-doctor practice has grown into a trusted healthcare destination serving patients across Fredericksburg, Spotsylvania, and Stafford.

We believe that true health comes from within. Rather than simply masking symptoms with medication, our approach focuses on identifying and correcting the underlying causes of pain and dysfunction. Through precise spinal adjustments, patient education, and personalized treatment plans, we empower our patients to take an active role in their own wellness.

Over the past 15 years, we have had the privilege of helping thousands of patients — from newborns to grandparents, weekend warriors to professional athletes — achieve better health and a higher quality of life. Every patient who walks through our doors becomes part of the Milestone family.

Our Philosophy

We practice what we call "whole-person chiropractic." That means we do not just look at the area that hurts — we assess your entire musculoskeletal system, your lifestyle, your daily habits, and your health goals. This comprehensive approach allows us to create treatment plans that deliver results that last, not just quick fixes that fade.

We also believe in transparency. We explain every diagnosis, every treatment option, and every cost before we begin. No surprises, no pressure — just honest care from people who genuinely want to help you feel your best.

Meet the Doctors

Dr. Sarah Mitchell, DC

Lead Chiropractor & Clinic Director

Dr. Mitchell earned her Doctor of Chiropractic degree from Palmer College of Chiropractic and has been practicing since 2007. She founded Milestone Chiropractic Centre in 2009 with the vision of creating a practice where patients feel heard, respected, and empowered.

Her clinical interests include spinal rehabilitation, sports injury recovery, and family wellness care. She is certified in Diversified Technique, Thompson Drop, and Activator Methods. Outside the clinic, Dr. Mitchell enjoys hiking the trails around Fredericksburg, coaching youth soccer, and spending time with her family.

Dr. Meggen Hayes, DC

Chiropractor — By Appointment

Dr. Hayes joined Milestone Chiropractic Centre to expand the practice's prenatal and pediatric care offerings. She graduated from Life University and holds advanced certifications in the Webster Technique and pediatric chiropractic through the ICPA.

Her gentle, nurturing approach makes her a favorite among expecting mothers and young families. Dr. Hayes sees patients by appointment and works closely with local OB-GYNs and midwives to provide coordinated care during pregnancy and postpartum recovery.
