Our Chiropractic Services

Comprehensive care for every stage of life. From pain relief to peak performance, we have you covered.

⚖

Chiropractic Adjustments

Our core service. We use a variety of precise, hands-on adjustment techniques to restore proper spinal alignment, reduce nerve interference, and improve your body's ability to heal itself. Whether you are dealing with acute pain or seeking preventive wellness care, regular adjustments are the foundation of chiropractic health.

Back Pain & Sciatica

Lower back pain and sciatica are among the most common reasons patients visit our office. We identify the root cause — whether it is a herniated disc, muscle imbalance, or joint dysfunction — and create a targeted treatment plan that delivers lasting relief without surgery or medication.

Neck Pain & Headaches

Chronic neck pain, tension headaches, and migraines often originate from cervical spine misalignment or muscle tension. Our gentle cervical adjustments and soft tissue work address the source of your pain, reducing headache frequency and restoring comfortable neck mobility.

Get Help for Neck Pain →

Sports Injury Recovery

Whether you are a competitive athlete or a weekend warrior, sports injuries can sideline you from the activities you love. We combine chiropractic adjustments with rehabilitation exercises, stretching protocols, and performance optimization to get you back in the game faster and stronger.

Start Your Recovery →
🤰

Pregnancy Chiropractic

Prenatal chiropractic care supports a healthier, more comfortable pregnancy. Our Webster Technique-certified doctors provide safe, gentle adjustments that address pelvic alignment, reduce back pain, and may help optimize fetal positioning for a smoother delivery.

Learn About Prenatal Care →
👶

Pediatric Chiropractic

Children benefit from chiropractic care too. Using extremely gentle techniques appropriate for growing bodies, we help with colic, ear infections, growing pains, posture issues, and overall developmental wellness. Our kid-friendly environment puts young patients at ease.

Learn About Pediatric Care →
🚬

Auto Accident Injuries

Whiplash and other auto accident injuries require prompt, specialized care. We provide thorough evaluations, document injuries for insurance purposes, and deliver effective treatment plans to help you recover fully. Early intervention is key to preventing chronic pain.

Get Evaluated Today →
🧐

Posture Correction

Poor posture from desk work, phone use, or daily habits can lead to chronic pain and reduced mobility. Our posture correction programs include spinal adjustments, ergonomic advice, and targeted exercises to help you stand taller and move with less pain.

Improve Your Posture →

Wellness & Preventive Care

You do not have to be in pain to benefit from chiropractic care. Many of our patients visit regularly for wellness adjustments that maintain spinal health, boost immune function, improve sleep, and keep their bodies performing at their best — before problems arise.
